Create Visitor Pass

Step 1. To create a temporary share link, launch the Drivers menu by tapping the icon indicated below.

Step 2. Next, tap Create Visitor Pass.

Step 3. On the next window, simply enter in the email address, start date and duration. After you have entered all the information, tap Send Link.

Step 4. In the main Drivers menu, you will now see their email address followed by the date and time they have access to Double.

Step 5. Shortly thereafter, they will receive an email with a unique link that will automatically connect them to Double.
